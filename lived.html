On the other hand, mathematic has important status in science field. Mathematics is one of the main majors that every student has to study in natural science. Mathematics is the basement of the majors in natural science. Such like physics, astronomy and chemistry. Mathematics has big contribution on human development. All the things that had been invented in this world may relate to math. For example, computer was invented by using a lot of data. The math is full of your life, full of each corner in this world. Mathematical model is a kind of abstract thing. Mathematical model is used to establish the relationship between different by using model. Set up a mathematical model for a company is very helpful. The model shows the causal relationship between order, manufacture and selling. There was a very successful example that I knew in China. The thing that had affected me to choose the major happened in senior high school. In these three years, I have read a lot of Chinese novels. The reason is from the one of these novels that I had read. The protagonist is a man who is really good at science, such as math, physics and chemistry. Math is his best. In his second year in university, he did something is craze. The protagonist stays at a room for a month because of a promising that he gave to his friend. He was making an economic mathematical model in this month. He sleeps for only 4 hours a day. And the other 20 hours is for searching the source and make the model. In a month later, while he was sleeping, the model had sharked everybody in business field.
